ℹ Boss Wars — Game Mode Info

Boss Wars is a 3-player co-op raid mode where the team faces one of 5 epic bosses on a dedicated arena.

⚔ 5 bosses to face:

  • Tier 1 — Captain (Easy, 5 000 HP)
  • Tier 2 — Werewolf General (Medium, 8 000 HP)
  • Tier 3 — Warlord Archangel (Hard, 13 000 HP)
  • Tier 4 — Demon Prince Dracula (Extreme, 20 000 HP)
  • Tier 5 — Drakkonungen (Nightmare, 30 000 HP)

🌟 Pre-match prep:

  • Choose your hero (Gandulf, Legolus, Gimlu, Aragurn)
  • Pick 3 talents of 10 possible (HP/dmg/AS/crit/lifesteal/etc.)
  • Pick 4 items of 10 possible (Slayer Blade / Phoenix Amulet / etc.)

🧠 Boss mechanics:

  • Every boss has 3 unique skills with a red telegraph on the ground — dodge!
  • Bosses 1–3 have a phase 2 at 50% HP: knockback + 3s stun + new skills
  • Bosses 4–5 have a phase 2 at 30% HP: harder new skills (apocalypse mode)

🏆 Win/Loss:

  • Win = kill the boss before the whole raid group is dead
  • Loss = the whole raid group is wiped
  • No respawn during raid — stay alive
